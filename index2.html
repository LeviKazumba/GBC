OUR SERVICES

We offer a variety of services which are carried out by well-trained, equipped and motivated personnel. They are monitored on hourly basis by line supervisors. Our services are as follows:

01.
SECURITY GUARDS:

Static guarding is one of our expert fields, we can provide round the clock security for your premises, office blocks, industrial estates, factories, hospitals, schools, warehouses and building sites. All our security officers are fully trained, vetted, uniformed, and hold a PRISA Certificates. They can be utilized as commercial officers to protect staff, stock and premises or front of house to assist your customers or staff. Our approach is flexible, you may also want to use us as a backup facility, for when your existing staff are on holiday, sick or just as a temporary measure.
